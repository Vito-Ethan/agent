Agent

A clothing based ecommerce website that supports cryptographic payments

This is a CS 426 Senior Project in Computer Science, Spring 2023, at UNR, CSE Department

Team 47

Project Description

We are developing an e-commerce website centered around a specific brand that sells clothing. The clothing products cater to both men and women, with products such as hoodies, pants, t-shirts, and long sleeves. Our primary goals are to integrate Web3 payment options, create a modern UI, and integrate wallet functionality. The main users will be anyone interested in clothing, paying with crypto, and purchasing NFTs. The website will function like most ecommerce platforms that people are familiar with. For example, account creation, adding products to cart, navigating through product departments, checking out with stripe, paypal, or choice of crypto currency. In terms of wallet integration, we want to allow users to connect their personal wallets (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet) to interface with the ethereum network so they can purchase the brands NFTs. The technologies that we are currently using are NextJS (Uses React and Node.js), Tailwind, TypeScript, Framer Motion, Coinbase Commerce, Stripe, Sanity CMS, Prisma ORM, Vercel (website hosting and database), and Postgres.
